Listen & Repeat: Word Stress and Sentence Position

Download

The keyword in each pair of sentences comes at two different places, first toward the beginning of the sentence and then towards the end. Notice the difference in volume and pitch in these words depending on the sentence position. First you'll hear each pair sentences and then the keyword, then you'll hear again each sentence twice, repeat each time.


1.  New York is my favorite city. / My favorite city is New York.
2.  Last night I saw a great movie. / I saw a great movie last night.
3.  Tomorrow is Valentine’s Day. / Valentine’s Day is tomorrow.
4.  My camera works well in low light. / Last week I lost my camera.
5.  Shoes are cheaper online. / I need a new pair of shoes.
6.  Lately I’ve been really tired. / I’ve been really tired lately.
7.  This afternoon I was on call. / I went to a movie this afternoon.
8.  The dining room isn’t used much. / We rarely eat in the dining room.
9.  John said I could borrow his car. / I’m going to dinner tonight with John.
10.  Saturday I’m leaving for Cancun. / I have to work all day Saturday.
